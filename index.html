About Sigma Audley (SSA)

🧪 Quality Assurance

Every product we supply undergoes rigorous third-party testing by Janoshik Analytical, one of the most respected independent laboratories in the pharmaceutical industry. Our commitment to quality includes:

  • HPLC/MS purity verification (>95% purity guaranteed)
  • Potency testing for accurate dosage confirmation
  • Heavy metal screening for safety assurance
  • Microbial testing for sterility verification
  • Complete certificate of analysis for every batch
