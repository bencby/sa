Why the Name Confusion?

1. Similar Starting Name

Both companies start with "Sigma" - but this is where the similarity ends. "Sigma" is a common prefix in scientific companies, like the Greek letter Σ used in mathematics.

2. Search Engine Mix-ups

Search engines sometimes suggest "Sigma-Aldrich" when you type "Sigma Audley" due to the larger company's web presence. Always verify you're on the correct website.

3. Both Serve Research Markets

While both companies serve researchers, Sigma Audley specializes exclusively in cutting-edge peptide research, while Sigma-Aldrich offers general laboratory chemicals.

4. No Business Relationship

Sigma Audley™ has never been affiliated with, owned by, or partnered with Sigma-Aldrich® or Merck. We are completely independent companies.
